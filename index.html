What exactly is the free AI Audit?
+
The AI Audit is a ruthless 48-hour deep-dive into your business — your operations, revenue model, tech stack, and team. We deliver a ranked map of your highest-leverage AI opportunities with projected ROI for each. It costs you nothing and commits you to nothing. We do it because confident clients make the best partners.
How quickly will we see real results?
+
Most clients see measurable outcomes within the first 30 days of system launch. Our fastest client saw qualified leads flowing from the AI Revenue Engine within 18 hours of go-live. We build fast and we measure everything from day one.
Do we need a large tech team to work with you?
+
No. We are the tech team. Our engagements are designed to be fully turnkey — we handle the architecture, the build, the integrations, and the launch. You need someone who can answer questions and give feedback. That's it.
What size businesses do you work with?
+
We work exclusively with growth-stage businesses — typically $1M to $50M in revenue — where the leverage from AI systems is highest and where there's enough organizational velocity to scale the results quickly. If you're earlier or later stage, reach out and we'll tell you honestly whether we're the right fit.
What does an engagement cost?
+
Engagements are scoped after the audit, so pricing is bespoke. We charge a flat project fee for the build phase and an optional monthly retainer for ongoing optimization. We don't bill by the hour — we're focused on outcomes, not time. Typical engagements range from $15k to $75k depending on scope.
Do you guarantee results?
+
Yes. The KPIs we commit to in the growth plan are written into your MSA. If we miss the targets we've agreed to, we keep working until we hit them — at no additional cost. We only take on clients we're confident we can deliver for.
